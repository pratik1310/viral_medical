Acute appendicitis
Incidence:-
  • - Most common surgical condition in abdomen
  • - more commonly seen in second and third decades of life
  • - there is some relation between amount of lymphoid tissue in appendix and acute appendicitis
  • - In the middle of the second decade this is at the pick


    •  Aetiology :-
    1. Obstruction of the lumen - obstruction in lumen - obstruction in the wall - obstruction outside the wall
    Obstruction in the lumen :-
    (a).Faecolith ( one type of stone consists of inspissated faecal material, epithelial debris , bacteria and calcium phosphates) (b). Hyperplasia of submucosal lymphoid follicles ( Major cause) (c). Worms ( Hook worm, thread worm)
    Obstruction in the wall - stricture or neoplasms of which the carcinoid is the commonest
    Obstruction outside the wall - Adhesion and kinks are common in this group
    2. Diet - this disease is commonly seen in highly civilised life who eats fish, meat
    3. Social status - disease of aristocratic family - social class 1 and 2
    4. Familial susceptibility
